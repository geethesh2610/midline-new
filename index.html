About Us

CIMAT International, a dynamic manufacturer and supplier of cable laying equipments. Established in 2017, we have rapidly emerged as a trusted name in the industry, providing innovative solutions for cable installation projects. We design, manufacture, and deliver top notch cable laying equipment that meets diverse needs of our clients.

With years of experience and a dedicated team of professionals, we bring expertise and innovation to every aspect of our manufacturing process. From Cable laying Rollers, Cable drum transporting trailers and Cable Pulling Winch, our products are engineered with precision and reliability to ensure optimal performance in the field.

We are dedicated to continuous improvement and innovation. Through ongoing research and development, we stay ahead of the curve, incorporating the latest technologies and trends into our products. This allows us to offer cutting -edge solutions that drive efficiency and performance in cable laying operations.
